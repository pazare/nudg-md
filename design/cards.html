  1. Context row. Type chip (color-coded), tempo mode, and always a patient chip — a nudge about nobody is not a nudge.
  2. Headline. The entire point in ≤ 90 characters, formal and verb-forward. A clinician who reads nothing else has been served.
  3. Evidence bullets. Two to four, each with a provenance chip (chart item, guideline, article). No unsourced sentences.
  4. Actions. Explicit verbs; the primary action is the safest useful one. Dismiss is a menu that collects a reason and arms the cooldown (2×→6×).
  5. Trace row. “Why this, why now” — triggering event, rule id, times shown. Every card is auditable; every notification traces to a condition code.
